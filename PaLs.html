About PaLs

History

PaLs is an online organisation established in 2016 by a group of people, at the time known as LapisArmy, to try and create a safe, friendly, and active social environment for people who liked to play video games. As the organisation developed the interests became universal, and people interested in a variety of topics were involved in the organisations development too.

The organisation has been through many phases throughout the years, but it has always managed to maintain it's online communal presence. The organisations most notable phase was it's involvement in Micronationalism, where The PaLsian Federation was established. This phase is where the organisations iconic logo/flag came from, which is still in use today.

PaLs Projects

Discord Server

Discord has played an important role in the development of PaLs, not only was PaLs first established on Discord, but it's Official Discord Server acts as a central hub for the organisation, all members are found here, and this is where most chat activity takes place.

Minecraft Server

PaLs is infamous for it's Minecraft activity, and more specifically it's minecraft server. Dating back to 2015, it has seen many updates, many users, and above all many contributor. Starting on an old laptop from 2013, to it's current location on a second hand gaming PC, it has never failed to deliver enjoyment to the people who log onto it, it has also never failed to preserve history, with build, items, and stories which date back to it's launch in 2015.
